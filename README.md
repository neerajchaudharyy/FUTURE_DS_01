# FUTURE_DS_01

🍫 KitKat Global Sales Performance Dashboard – 2022

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of KitKat’s global sales performance for the year 2022. The report delivers actionable business insights by analyzing revenue trends, product performance, country-wise contribution, and salesperson efficiency.

📊 Project Overview

The dashboard highlights key performance indicators including:

Total Sales: 6.18M

Average Sales Value: 5.65K

Total Quantity Sold: 177K

Total Products: 22

It enables stakeholders to monitor sales trends, identify top-performing regions and products, and evaluate overall business health.

🌍 Key Insights
🔹 Regional Performance

Australia leads with 1.14M in sales.

UK, India, USA, and Canada show balanced contributions.

Revenue distribution across countries reduces regional dependency risk.

🔹 Product Performance

Sales contribution is evenly distributed among top products.

No single product dominates revenue, indicating diversified portfolio strength.

🔹 Monthly Sales Trend

Strong performance in January (896K).

Noticeable dip in April followed by recovery in June.

Possible seasonal demand impact observed.

🔹 Top Salespersons

The dashboard also identifies the top 5 performing sales representatives, helping in performance evaluation and incentive planning.
